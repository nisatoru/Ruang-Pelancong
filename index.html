Trip overview

This three day program connects Semarang, Jakarta and Bandung through a compact educational itinerary. Participants gather at Muhammadiyah University of Semarang then travel by bus to Jakarta for a day of fun at Dufan and Ancol Beach. On the second day the group continues to Bandung to experience Cibaduyut Market, Braga Street, Subang and Lembang. The last day focuses on souvenirs and the return journey to Semarang.

The itinerary below is taken from a real campus program and rewritten in English. It can be used by students as material to practice tour commentary, giving information about timing and places, and handling a group schedule in English.
