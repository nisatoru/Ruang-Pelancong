Trip overview

This 3 day student trip connects Jakarta and Bandung in one compact journey. The program is designed for campus groups who want to enjoy amusement parks, heritage streets, shopping areas and cool highland scenery in a single package.

On the first day you travel from Semarang to Jakarta to experience Dufan and Ancol. The second day focuses refreshing on Kawah Putih Ciwidey and shopping in Cibaduyut, also enjoying on Braga Street. On the last day you shop for local snacks before returning to campus.

Short day by day itinerary

Day 01: Jakarta - Dufan and Ancol
Fun day in the capital city

After gathering at the campus, you travel to Jakarta with breakfast and lunch stops at highway rest areas. In the afternoon you arrive at Dufan to enjoy the rides, then continue to Ancol beach area for photos and relaxing. In the evening the group has dinner around Ancol before heading to the hotel for check in and rest.

Day 02: Bandung - Kawah Putih - Cibaduyut - Braga Street
Shopping, heritage street and highland atmosphere

> “After breakfast and check-out, the journey continues to Bandung via the Cipularang toll road. Participants visit Kawah Putih, followed by shopping at Cibaduyut. After lunch, the group explores Braga Street for a short heritage walk and photo session. In the evening, the group has dinner in the Lembang area, checks in to the hotel, and rests for the night.”

Day 03: Souvenirs and return trip
Snacks, rest area and arrival back at campus

In the morning you have breakfast and check out from the hotel, then visit a souvenir center such as Kartika Sari or Prima for snack shopping. After a break and lunch at a rest area the group returns to Semarang. The journey ends at the university with a short closing, documentation and then students go back home.
