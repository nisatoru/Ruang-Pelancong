Short Day-by-Day Itinerary

DAY 01 – Jakarta (Dufan & Ancol)
Travel from campus, lunch at rest area, enjoy rides at Dufan followed by sunset at Ancol beach. Dinner and check-in hotel.

DAY 02 – Bandung (Cibaduyut–Braga–Ciater)
Depart to Bandung, shopping at Cibaduyut, lunch at Saung Kabayan, heritage walk at Braga Street, sunset at Ciater, dinner at Lembang, hotel check-in.

DAY 03 – Souvenir & Return
Visit Kartika Sari/Prima souvenir center then travel back to Semarang, closing remarks and documentation.

Trip Highlights

  • Amusement park experience at Jakarta's iconic Dufan
  • Seaside relaxation and photography at Ancol
  • Leather shopping experience at famous Cibaduyut street
  • Heritage spot exploration at Braga Street
  • Cool mountain weather in Ciater and Lembang
  • Educational group bonding through shared agenda
